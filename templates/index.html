What is FairShare?

FairShare is an easy-to-use calculator that can help figure out how much you and your friends split a bunch of group expenses! Have a group of friends that you've been hanging out with for months and can't figure out how much you have to pay each other back? Just create a group, enter the people and the payments, and FairShare can calculate it all for you!
